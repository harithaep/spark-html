FAQs

1. What is included in the free trial?

The free trial includes 500 reports that you can generate using SPARK.ai. With no upfront commitment, your team can experience all key features, including intelligent reporting, AI-driven automation, and PACS integration.

2. How does SPARK.ai improve reporting accuracy?

SPARK.ai leverages advanced AI to minimize grammar, spelling, and formatting errors while ensuring accurate dictation placement in predefined templates. Its real-time support ensures your reports meet the highest standards of precision.

3. Can SPARK.ai integrate with my existing PACS system?

Yes, SPARK.ai seamlessly integrates with your PACS system, syncing worklists and eliminating the need for manual data entry, allowing you to streamline your workflow.

4. What does the onboarding process look like?

Our onboarding process is simple and efficient. We guide you step-by-step through uploading templates, setting up macros, and integrating them with your existing systems. Expert support is available throughout to ensure a smooth transition.

5. How does the referral program work?

When you refer a colleague who signs up and starts using SPARK.ai, you will receive 500 free reports as a reward. *Only happens when the referred person signs up and starts payments
